Blue Arrow awards selects the best services, teams, people and code in digital industry in 8 categories.

We are looking for quality and depth in concept, design and code.

Schedule

The competition is open for entries from 25.1.2016 to 29.2.2016. Enter your product, service or campaign if you feel it has made a real impact.

Gala

The award gala will take place in 14.4.2016 at the Savoy theatre, Helsinki

Jury

Entries in each category will be judged by some of the most fascinating and skilled names in their respective fields. The jury will be announced in December 2015.

Categories

The categories will be published in December 2015.
